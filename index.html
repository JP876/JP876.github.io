Goal of this website

People have always been fascinated by robots like those from movies, for example Star Wars or the Wall-E. Therefore, the goal of this site is to show in a simple way how robots generally function by combining acquired knowledge and practice.

Learn about robotics

Robotics is a very broad science so some knowledge is needed to make the topic easier to understand, and apply it more efficiently in practice.

Build projects

The best way to really learn something is through some form of practice. To achieve this, is to use previously acquired knowledge to make some projects.

Inspire others

Once you are done with the project, feel free to share with others. This way you motivate others to continue studying the interesting world of robotics

About robotics

Robotics is the study of robots. Robots are machines that can be used to do jobs. Some robots can do work by themselves. Other robots must always have a person telling them what to do.

Electronic engineering

Electronic engineering is a form of engineering associated with electronic circuits, devices and the equipment and systems that use them.

Mechanical engineering

Mechanical engineering is the application of the principles and problem-solving techniques of engineering from design to manufacturing to the marketplace for any object.

Information engineering

Information engineering is a software engineering approach to designing and developing information systems.
Information technology engineering involves an architectural approach for planning, analyzing, designing, and implementing applications.

Computer science

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. Unlike electrical and computer engineers, computer scientists deal mostly with software and software systems; this includes their theory, design, development, and application.

About

We are a small team that is passionate about robotics and its progress. We believe that robots can help people in their daily tasks and improve living conditions. We also believe that robots cannot replace human interaction and that this is not their main purpose. As robots start to appear more and more often, we created this page to make as many people as possible familiar with the world of robotics.
